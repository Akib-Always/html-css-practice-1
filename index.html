Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovaton

Innovation means creating new ideas, improving technology, solving problems, and making life easier through creativity, knowledge, and modern thinking.

Sustainability

Sustainability means using natural resources wisely, protecting environment, reducing waste, and ensuring future generations can live healthy lives on Earth.

Infrastructure

Infrastructure includes roads, bridges, electricity, water systems, internet, and buildings that support economic growth, transportation, communication, and daily life.

Empowerment

Empowerment means giving people confidence, knowledge, resources, and opportunities to make decisions, improve lives, and achieve their goals independently.

"Dr. Muhammad Yunus is a renowned Bangladeshi economist, social entrepreneur, and the founder of Grameen Bank. He is the pioneer of the concept of microcredit, which has brought transformative change in poverty alleviation. In 1983, he established Grameen Bank to provide microloans to poor individuals who were excluded from traditional financial services. Dr. Yunus and Grameen Bank jointly received the Nobel Peace Prize in 2006 for their efforts to create economic and social development from below. He is also a leading advocate of "social business," focusing on sustainable solutions to global issues. Dr. Yunus remains a symbol of human development and financial inclusion worldwide."
